Airing Mental Health: A Global Social Issue

Awareness Page

Mental Health problems do not choose a particular age,
they can be experienced by everyone wherever they may be
in the world. It has become an issue because of its effects
on people which is why we should be aware of this issue,
open our eyes, and take this issue seriously. It may look
harmless on the outside but its effects are very concerning.

Causes

There are many reasons why people experience mental health problems.
Let us learn more about these causes to understand the people undergoing
mental health problems in order to understand them more.

Effects

Mental health problems affect many areas and not just the person
experiencing them. Let us learn more about the effects of mental
health problems for us to be more aware.
